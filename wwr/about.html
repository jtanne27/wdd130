About Full Send Rafting Co.

At Full Send Rafting Co., we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable white water rafting experiences. Our team of expert guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety while delivering an adrenaline-pumping adventure on some of the most exciting rivers. Founded in 2010, Full Send Rafting Co. has grown from a small local operation to a renowned rafting company known for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We believe that every trip should be an opportunity to connect with nature, challenge yourself, and create lasting memories. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, we offer a variety of trips tailored to different skill levels. Join us for an adventure of a lifetime!

Our History

Full Send Rafting Co. was founded in 2010 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for white water rafting with others. Starting with just a few rafts and a small team of guides, the company has grown significantly over the years, expanding its fleet and offering trips on some of the most exciting rivers in the region.

We have always prioritized safety, environmental stewardship, and customer satisfaction. Our guides are highly trained and experienced, ensuring that every trip is both thrilling and safe. We are proud to have built a loyal customer base and a reputation for excellence in the rafting community.
