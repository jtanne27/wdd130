Choose Your Adventure!

Ready to book your next rafting trip? Contact Us today for more information and reservations!

Family Rafting Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this trip offers gentle rapids, beautiful scenery, and plenty of fun. Enjoy a half-day float with experienced guides and all equipment provided.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Minimum Age: 6

Extreme Rapids Challenge

For thrill-seekers! Tackle class IV and V rapids on this full-day adventure. Includes lunch, safety gear, and expert instruction. Previous rafting experience recommended.

  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Minimum Age: 16

Scenic River Expedition

Immerse yourself in nature on this relaxing expedition. Enjoy wildlife sightings, swimming stops, and a riverside picnic. Great for photographers and nature lovers.

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Minimum Age: 10
